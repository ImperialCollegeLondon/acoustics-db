Welcome to Rainforest Rhythms

We are scientists working in some of the world's most threatened rainforest. As part of our research, we collect recordings of the day to day sounds of the rainforest: these soundscapes help us learn about the species that live in the forest and the overall health of the forest.

On this site, you can listen to these soundscapes. You can also help us by finding animal calls in our recordings and - if you think you know what made the call - telling us what it is.
